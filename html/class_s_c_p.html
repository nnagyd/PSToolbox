Detailed Description

Class to model a slightly compressible pipe.

A class that is used for modelling a Slightly Compressible Pipe. (The latter means that the liquid is treated as having constant density, and the speed of sound is much higher than the usual flow speeds in the pipe.) The solution of the system will be based on the method of characterisctics (MOC)
